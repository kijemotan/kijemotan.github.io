*soft letters

softenings

  • Дь/Д[ё] - Dź/Dzi
  • Зь/З[ё] - Ź/Zi
  • Ль/Л[ё] - L
  • Нь/Н[ё] - Ń/Ni
  • Рь/Р[ё] - Rz
  • Сь/С[ё] - Ś/Si
  • Ть/Т[ё] - Ć/Ci

note: if the last letter of a consonant cluster is softened, the entire cluster is softened, e.g. стя̨гать /ˈɕt͡ɕɔŋ.ɡat͡ɕ/
